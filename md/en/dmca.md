# DMCA Policy — Petit Meme

**Last updated: March 10, 2026**

## Purpose

Petit Meme is a platform for indexing and sharing video memes. We do not own the rights to the referenced videos. In accordance with the Digital Millennium Copyright Act (17 U.S.C. § 512), we respect intellectual property rights and respond to legitimate takedown notifications.

## DMCA Notification Procedure

If you are a copyright owner (or an authorized representative) and believe that content hosted or referenced on Petit Meme infringes your rights, you may send us a DMCA notification containing the following elements:

1. **Identification of the copyrighted work** — a description of the work you claim has been infringed.
2. **Identification of the infringing content** — URL or sufficient description to locate the content on Petit Meme.
3. **Your contact information** — name, mailing address, phone number and email address.
4. **Good faith statement** — a statement that you believe in good faith that the use of the content is not authorized by the copyright owner, its agent or the law.
5. **Statement under penalty of perjury** — a statement, under penalty of perjury, that the information in the notification is accurate and that you are the copyright owner or are authorized to act on their behalf.
6. **Signature** — a physical or electronic signature of the copyright owner or their authorized representative.

## Counter-Notification

If you believe your content was removed in error or due to misidentification, you may send us a counter-notification containing:

1. **Identification of the removed content** — a description of the removed content and its location prior to removal.
2. **Statement under penalty of perjury** — a statement, under penalty of perjury, that you believe in good faith that the content was removed or disabled as a result of mistake or misidentification.
3. **Your contact information** — name, mailing address, phone number and email address.
4. **Consent to jurisdiction** — a statement that you consent to the jurisdiction of the federal court in your district and that you will accept service of process from the original complainant.
5. **Signature** — a physical or electronic signature.

Upon receipt of a valid counter-notification, we will forward a copy to the original complainant. If the complainant does not file a court action within 10 to 14 business days, we may restore the content.

## Repeat Infringer Policy

Petit Meme reserves the right to suspend or terminate accounts of users who are subject to repeated DMCA notifications.
